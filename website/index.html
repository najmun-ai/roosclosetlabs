Product 1 — Return Intelligence
MIRROR

Causal Return
Intelligence

Scores every order at checkout in under 150ms. Explains the causal root of potential returns — not just the probability. Prescribes interventions with ROI estimates so your team acts on findings, not dashboards.

The fashion industry has return prediction tools. None explain why returns happen or what to do about it. MIRROR is built on DoWhy causal inference — the same framework used for causal reasoning in academic research — trained on your returns data to identify true causal roots versus correlations.

When MIRROR says adding a size chart will reduce returns by 12%, it's a backdoor-adjusted causal estimate, not a correlation. That distinction is the difference between a dashboard and an intelligence system.

<150ms
Return risk scoring at checkout — XGBoost on 200 features across 4 causal domains
200
Features per order: sizing signals, content quality, customer history, order context
3
Processing layers: predict risk → explain causal roots → prescribe ranked interventions
Layer 01
Predict

XGBoost scores return probability at checkout using 200 features across sizing signals, content quality, customer history, and order context. Sub-150ms P99 latency. Runs synchronously at the order API boundary.

Real-time · XGBoost · SageMaker
Layer 02
Explain

DoWhy causal graph runs async on high-risk orders. Identifies true causal roots with backdoor adjustment for confounders. Outputs: sizing uncertainty (47%), photography mismatch (31%), description gap (15%), logistics signals (7%).

Async · DoWhy · Kinesis
Layer 03
Prescribe

Bedrock Claude generates ranked interventions with ROI estimates specific to your SKU and volume. Tracks outcomes for continuous model improvement. Surfaces in embedded QuickSight dashboard for merchandising teams.

Async · Bedrock Claude · QuickSight

Product 2 — Civic Technology

BoroBhai
Bengali Civic AI

Government processes are hard. Harder still when your language isn't the one the forms were designed in. BoroBhai gives every Bengali-speaking citizen access to an expert who speaks their language.

Ask in Bengali or Banglish. BoroBhai retrieves the right procedure from a knowledge base of 179 expert-reviewed civic workflows, generates the document you need, compresses or merges the files you have, and transcribes your voice if you'd rather not type.

Built as a pro bono civic technology project. Freemium business model with a paid tier for power users. Serverless architecture keeps the cost of serving one citizen at fractions of a cent.
