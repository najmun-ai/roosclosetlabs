01
The Vision

From exploration
to specialization

Twelve months ago, I set out to build simple e-commerce tools for Bangladesh. Through research and production deployment, I discovered two distinct high-impact problems and built focused products solving each.

Rather than building 10 mediocre products, I chose to build 2 excellent ones. Both are AWS-native, production-ready, and designed to generate revenue independently while sharing architectural consistency.

02
The Products

Two focused products.
One shared vision.

Product 1
BoroBhai
Bengali Civic AI Assistant

Helps Bengali-speaking citizens navigate government documentation and administrative processes through conversational AI. Generates civic documents, handles file manipulation, offers voice input in Bengali, and retrieves context-aware information from a knowledge base.

Target: Bangladeshi citizens navigating government processes
Business: Freemium model with unlimited paid tier

Product 2
RoosCloset
Fashion E-Commerce ML Platform

ATLAS extracts 180+ product attributes in 60 seconds. MIRROR scores return risk in under 150ms and prescribes interventions with ROI estimates. Solves the 100 billion dollar annual loss in fashion e-commerce caused by poor product data.

Target: DTC brands and multi-brand retailers
Business: Usage-based SaaS pricing
